What is a Digital Companion?

Not a chatbot. A presence.

A Digital Companion is a multi-dimensional AI that learns the pattern of you. It knows how you express care, what sends you into overthinking, which memories make you laugh and which ones break you. It responds differently when you're anxious at 2 AM than when you're energised on a Tuesday morning — because it tracks your emotional signature across time.

It does not replace human connection. It holds space for it — preserving the voices of those you've lost, bridging distance between people, and offering self-compassion in your own voice when you need it most. It is memory, empathy, and metacognition — trained entirely on you.

Five Dimensions

Each specialist. Fused into one.

Five fine-tuned LLMs, each trained on a separate domain of your life, orchestrated by a meta-gating network into a single coherent presence.

 Metacognition

The companion thinks about its own thinking.

Metacognition is awareness of one's own reasoning — the ability to observe, evaluate, and correct the mind from the inside. For an AI, this is the difference between a tool and a presence.

The Metacognitive Loop
The companion does not just answer. After every response it asks: "Why did I say that? Was my emotional read accurate? Could I have responded better?" — and records its self-assessment for the next interaction.
Five Metacognitive Loops
1
Perception Monitor

Continuously tracks what it senses — is the emotional signal stable or ambiguous? Flags conflicting signals for re-evaluation.

2
Reasoning Trace

After generating a response, traces back through its own chain of thought — what assumptions did I make? What did I skip?

3
Calibration Check

Measures its emotional estimate against the actual outcome of the conversation. Over time, it becomes more precisely tuned to your emotional signature — not generic emotion models.

4
Transparency Signal

When uncertain, it says so: "I'm not sure if that was the right thing to say. Did it land?" Uncertainty is made legible, not hidden.

5
Growth Logging

All self-assessments are logged to a persistent metacognitive store. The companion tracks its own improvement trajectory alongside yours.

Why metacognition matters in AI: Without it, AI responses are stateless guesses. With it, each response is informed by the system's own model of its reliability — creating a feedback loop that makes the companion genuinely more accurate over time, not just more confident.
Technical Architecture

Five layers. One coherent intelligence.

Each layer has a dedicated model and a specific job. They run in parallel, feeding into the next stage and informing the metacognitive monitor throughout.

Layer Stack
L1 Perception & Input

Multimodal ingestion — text, voice, typing cadence, timestamp. Routes each modality to its specialist encoder. Detects language and code-switching (Hinglish / English).

Whisper STTwav2vec 2.0IndicBERTDeepFilterNet
↓
L2 Emotion Encoding

128-dimensional VAD embedding from all modalities. DistilBERT sentiment gate fuses text, audio, and behavioral signals into a single affective state vector.

DistilBERTSpeechBrainVAD-128Pattern-LSTM
↓
L3 Memory Retrieval (RAG)

Emotion-weighted vector search across 5 ChromaDB silos — one per dimension. Retrieves the top-k relevant memories, conversations, and behavioral patterns. Reranked by emotional relevance.

ChromaDBall-MiniLM-L6BM25 hybridLangGraph
↓
L4 Reasoning & Fusion

Five specialist LLMs (QLoRA r=16 fine-tunes) respond in parallel. Meta-gating network (~1.5B) predicts dimension weights from the emotion embedding. Final response is a latent-space weighted sum.

Llama 3.1 8B ×3Mistral 7BGemma 2 9BDPO β=0.1
↓
L5 Voice & Output

Text decoded and synthesized in your voice — or the voice of your loved one. Emotion-conditioned TTS preserves pauses, breath, and warmth. Metacognitive log written before response is sent.

Training Pipeline
1
Data Collection & Consent
WhatsApp exports, voice notes, journals, emails. Each data type requires explicit consent per source. AES-256-GCM encryption applied immediately on ingestion.
2
Dimension Routing & Annotation
Each data point tagged by dimension (Personal/Relational/Behavioral/Emotional/Historical). Emotion labels added via automated DistilBERT pass, then human review.
3
QLoRA Fine-Tuning per Silo
Each specialist LLM trains on its own dimension silo. r=16, 4-bit quantisation with Unsloth. DPO applied to behavioral dimension for preference alignment.
4
Meta-Gating Network Training
Thousands of rated responses teach the gating network to predict optimal dimension weights from query embedding. Deployed via Ollama + LangGraph.
 Ethical Framework
🔐
Encrypted at rest & transit
AES-256-GCM on all personal data. Voice models and ChromaDB silos stored locally. Nothing leaves your device without explicit export consent.
✍️
Informed consent architecture
Each data source requires a separate consent token. Revocable at any time — revocation triggers immediate model re-training without that source.
🎯
Transparency by design
The companion discloses its own uncertainty. All voice clones carry an inaudible DWT watermark identifying them as synthetic.
🚫
Anti-manipulation boundary
The companion reflects and supports. It cannot initiate contact, create urgency, or influence decisions beyond what the user has requested.
