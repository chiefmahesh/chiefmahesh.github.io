Neural TTS + Emotional AI

Coqui TTS with emotion-embedding (anger, love, nostalgia). Style-encoder trained on 10h of personal speech — preserves breathing rhythm, laughter, pauses.

Example: Coqui TTS with emotion-embedding. Style-encoder on 10h personal speech.

Best tech: Azure Neural TTS, ElevenLabs v2 for prosody, or open-source XTTS-v2 with emotion heads.

⚡ Technical Suggestions to Improve Efficiency

☁️
NeRF Streaming Compression

3D Gaussian splatting (real-time) with 10× less compute. InstantNGP avatars under 10 MB.

🎛️
Emotion VAE Adapter

Small VAE on your voice to smoothly interpolate arousal/valence. Saves fine-tuning multiple models.

📈
Hybrid RAG-LLM

MemoryBank with daily diary retrieval. Augment with last 10 interactions for coherent personality.

📸
Diffusion-Based Super-Res

After 3DMM render, apply GFPGAN or CodeFormer for 4 K skin details without full re-render.

💾
TinyML Edge Sentiment

On-device (Raspberry Pi 5) distilled DistilBERT detects family mood before response generation.

🔗
Multimodal Sync Loss

SyncNet + emotion consistency loss during training avoids "dead eyes" or mismatched laugh.

⚡
Caching Frequent Replies

Precompute 200 most probable legacy answers (birthday, "I miss you") for low-latency serving.

🌐
Hinglish Code-Switch Model

Fine-tune smaller Hindi-LLM (OpenHathi) with family Hinglish corpus for natural language mix.
